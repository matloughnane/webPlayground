Visit Us

We are easy to find once you get to the island. Only a short walk from the ferry pier you are as good as at the bar once you step on the island. Below is some useful information on getting to the island and an interactive location map showing where we are.

There are regular ferry services to the island from Burtonport starting early in the morning and running almost hourly throughout the day. Timetables can be found at the harbour in Burtonport, or you can contact the ferry operators directly for more information.
